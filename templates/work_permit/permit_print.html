SECTION 6 — RENEWAL WORKFLOW AUTHORIZATION

Guidelines for Renewal of Work Permit

Work permit needs to be renewed:

  • At start of General shift every day.
  • Whenever initiator is changed for any reason.
  • Whenever Co-Permittee is changed for any reason.

Procedure for renewal of Work permit:

  1. Initiator shall brief risk assessment and its mitigation to work group and obtain signature of Co-permittee.
  2. Issuer shall sign on both copies (1st and 2nd copy) of permit, return 1st copy to initiator and retain 2nd copy with him.
  3. Initiator shall restart work after renewal by issuer.
  4. In the event of change in work group, Renewal of Permit from issuer is not required. However, Initiator should explain safety measures, risk assessment and its mitigation to new person(s) in the work group and should sign and obtain signature of co-permittee.

While permit is due for renewal, and / or is under renewal process, Initiator shall not start work.
